My Favorite Animal: Black Panther

Reasons I love Panthers

  • Great Swimmers
  • Capable of leaping up to 20 feet
  • The gestation period of Panthers is 3 to 4 months
  • Panthers like to dive to relax themselves.

History

In 1788, Jean-Claude Delamétherie described a black leopard that was kept in the Tower of London and had been brought from Bengal. In 1794, Friedrich Albrecht Anton Meyer proposed the scientific name Felis fusca for this individual, the Indian leopard. In 1809, Georges Cuvier described a black leopard kept in the Ménagerie du Jardin des plantes that had been brought from Java. Cuvier proposed to name it Felis melas, the Javan leopard.
In 1801, Félix de Azara described a black jaguar observed by local people near the Paraná River in Paraguay.
